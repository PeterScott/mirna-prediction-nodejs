pre-miRNA Predictor

Enter one or more sequences in FASTA format, and the predictor will run Triplet-SVM and MiPred to determine whether or not each sequence is likely to be a pre-miRNA. Because MiPred is much slower than Triplet-SVM, no attempt is made to combine their results into an ensemble classifier; however, their results will be shown side-by-side for comparison.
